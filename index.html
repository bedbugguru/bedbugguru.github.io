bed bug guru was formed in early 2022 by Jake Knoechel (formerly of Hensley) and Shane Malone while studying at the University of Florida. After cutting their teeth in different Jacksonville indie pop outfits, the duo wanted to explore a heavier, fuzzier sound with a focus on live performance. Once Shane found the name "Bed Bug Guru" embroidered on a shirt at a thrift store, the band began exchanging demos and quickly recruited friend and roommate Cleburne Wilson to play drums. After briefly playing with long-time friend and bassist Clay Dixon, the band recruited fellow Hensley alumnus Kendall Mason for bass and vocals. The lineup was solidified after photographer and videographer Jake Faiella joined the band to shoot their Halloween tour. His psychedelic projections have become an essential element of the band's performance. bed bug guru is currently working on their debut album.
