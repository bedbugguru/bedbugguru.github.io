fuzz rock band from gainesville, fl

bed bug guru was formed in early 2022 by Jake Knoechel (formerly of Hensley) and Shane Malone) while studying at the University of Florida. After cutting their teeth in different Jacksonville indie pop outfits, the duo wanted to explore a heavier, fuzzier sound with a focus on live performance. Once Shane found the name "Bed Bug Guru" embroidered on a shirt at a thrift store, the band began exchanging demos and quickly recruited friend and roommate Cleburne Wilson to play drums. The lineup was solidified after fellow Hensley alumnus Kendall Mason joined the band on bass/vocals in Fall 2022.
